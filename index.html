Have Questions?

We've Got You Covered

Who can attend Luxe Living Summit?

The summit is open to developers, investors, hospitality brands, designers, and service providers in the luxury real estate space. All attendees are vetted to ensure a qualified audience.

How do I qualify for a 1:1 meeting pod?

Meeting pods are available to sponsors and premium delegates. You can apply during registration, and our team will review your profile to ensure productive matchmaking.

Can international brands participate?

Absolutely! We welcome global luxury brands looking to enter or expand in the Indian market. Special concierge services are available for international attendees.

How do I nominate a speaker or apply for awards?

Speaker nominations and award applications can be submitted through our website. The selection committee reviews all submissions on a rolling basis.

What's the dress code or onsite setup?

The event is business formal. The venue offers premium meeting pods, lounge areas, and showcase spaces designed for luxury networking.
